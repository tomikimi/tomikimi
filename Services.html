Service Schedule

At the heart of the life of the Church of the Resurrection 1004 is the daily pattern of prayer and worship. This daily rythm of prayer forms the framework of all that we do.


We begin and end the day with prayer,Music is integral to the worshipinng and life of our church.
You are always welcome here, whoever you are and whatever you do.

Discover more about our regular services
  • Monday to Friday - Early Morning Prayer and Holy Communion, 7.00am
  • First & Third Sunday - 7.30 am Holy Communion Service, 10.00 am Holy Communion Service(Choral Communion Setting)
  • Second Sunday -7.30 am Teaching Sermon/Serminar(Choral Mattins and Anointing Service),10.00 am Teaching Sermon/Serminar(Choral Mattins and Anointing Service)
  • Fourth Sunday - 7.30 am Holy Communion Service, 10.00 am Mattins/Praise and Worship
  • Fifth Sunday - 7.30 am  and 10.00 am Youth Sunday(Youth Seminar Talk/Praise and Worship)
  • Even Song - 5.00pm Every Sunday (Hosted by Church Societies)
