Who We Are

The Vision and Values of the Church of the Resurrection Victoria Island

Our Vision

  1. Church of theResurrection 1004 Victoria Island seeks to enable people in all their diversity to encounter the Resurrection power of God in Christ Jesus.
  2. As a Family of God,we work with care and imagination to be a centre for welcome, worship and learning which inspires successive generations to engage with the richness of the chritian faith and its heritage



Our Values

  1. Love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, generosity, faithfulness, gentleness and self-control.
  2. To Uphold integrity, honesty and openness in what we do.
  3. To aim for the highest possible standards in everything we do, acknowledging that we cannot do everything.
