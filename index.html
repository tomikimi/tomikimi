OUR MISSION

This ANGLICAN CHURCH is here to:

  1. Proclaim the Good News of redemption from the guilt, penalty, dominion and pollution of sin, solely through the sacrificial death (as our Representative and Substitute) of the Lord Jesus Christ, the incarnate Son of God.
